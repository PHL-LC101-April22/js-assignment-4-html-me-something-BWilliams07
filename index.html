My True Love - Baking!

Love at first bite

I first discovered my love for baking when I was in college. I remember I had a very stressful semester and I needed a hobby for some relaxation. It was in the middle of Fall so I couldn't spend my time on the beach - "wastin' away again in Margaritaville". I desperately needed something else to do. I was tired of spending my free time at the gym so I figured baking would be the next best thing! *Who says you can't have margaritas and cupcakes?!

Baking is creative!


A lot of people associate baking with either being boring or being like Betty Crocker and Martha Stewart - (minus the stock fraud that is!). Believe it or not, baking is creative and every creative hobby releases endorphines in your brain! I liked that every time I was stressed I could just look up a recipe and lose myself in the kitchen. For a momment in time all of my problems no longer existed. The only thing I had to worry about was not burning down the house! (never a dull moment there!)

Reasons I picked up baking as a hobby:

  1. You can be as creative as you want, there's no limit!
  2. You can eat the finished result!
  3. Sweets also release endorphines, so double the dose of happy :)

Ehhhh or not so fast!

Through baking I discovered that I might actually have a creative side to my being! Ehhh or maybe not. While I mastered not burning the house down, there's still a lot of work to be done as far as creativity goes! I might not be able to create the best masterpieces yet, but baking continues to help me keep my sanity. Even if I'm no Marth stewart or Betty Crocker I will still always love baking! I won't have my own baking show one day but I definetly have the skills to star in the show "Nailed It!" (baking fails).
